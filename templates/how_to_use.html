How to Use HealthPredict

Follow these steps to get started with our AI-powered disease prediction system.

Step 1: Select Your Symptoms

Begin typing your symptoms and select from the suggestions that appear. Each selected symptom is automatically formatted with a comma for ease of input.

Step 2: Click 'Predict'

Press the 'Predict' button to let our AI analyze your symptoms and suggest possible diseases.

Step 3: Review the Prediction

You'll get a detailed breakdown including the predicted disease, description, precautions, medications, workouts, and diet recommendations.

Step 4: Take Action

Follow the provided precautions and recommendations. Remember, this is an AI-based prediction — always consult a medical professional for confirmation.
