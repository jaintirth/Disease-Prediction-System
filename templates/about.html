About HealthPredict

HealthPredict is an AI-powered disease prediction platform designed to help users identify potential health conditions based on their symptoms. Our goal is to make early detection and health guidance more accessible and user-friendly.

Our Mission

We aim to empower individuals with fast, accurate, and reliable health predictions, encouraging better awareness and timely action.

Why Choose HealthPredict?

  • Accurate AI predictions with Support Vector Classification (SVC)
  • Comprehensive coverage of 40+ diseases
  • Detailed symptom breakdowns, medications, diets, and workouts
  • Friendly, clean, and interactive user interface
